After five months of decline, the price of regular diesel has risen again.

Common diesel saw a slight increase of 0.16% in the first half of July, reaching an average price of R$ 6.16, according to the latest analysis from the Edenred Ticket Log Price Index (IPTL). This marks the first price hike since February and ends a series of declines driven by Petrobras' downward price adjustments throughout the first half of 2025. Frost events contribute to pest control in grape production.

Winter pruning of early-cycle grapevines has begun in the administrative region of Caxias do Sul, according to information released this Thursday (July 17) in the Emater/RS-Ascar Crop Bulletin. At the same time, winter fertilization is also underway, following technical recommendations for proper vineyard management. The agency highlighted that the frosts recorded in recent weeks have helped reduce the presence of pathogenic fungi and pests, while also acting as a natural mechanism for controlling invasive plants.
